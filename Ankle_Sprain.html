How to Treat Ankle Sprain

Ankle sprain

Credit:Joseph ibuyan
Link vid: https://youtu.be/FEMkcdPsaI8?si=6q3phozl5sL__FkF

Info:
- [ ] Place the center of the bandage under the arch of the foot.
- [ ] Bring one end over the top of the foot and around the ankle,
then bring it back under the foot and up again, creating a figure-eight pattern.
- [ ] Securely wrap the bandage around the ankle,
ensuring it's snug but not too tight to restrict circulation.
- [ ] Use the other end of the triangular bandage
to make additional turns around the ankle if necessary.
- [ ] Once wrapped, bring the ends of the bandage to the
front of the ankle and tie them in a secure knot.
