How To Survive in Fire

House fire
• Stay calm.
• Call firefighters.
• Alert everyone in the house.
• If toxic smoke starts to fill up the space,
then quickly soak a cloth with water and tie
it over your nose and mouth.

You’re in the room, and the door is closed.
• Check the door if it’s hot before opening it.
If it's hot, it means there’s a fire, and find another way.
• If it's cool, then open it slowly and check for smoke
or flames and move ahead by staying low.

Trapped in a room
• Put a towel or clothes under the door to stop
the smoke from coming in.
• Seal any gaps around the window with a wet towel or sheets.
Clothes catch fire.
• Use the safety technique called SDR, or stop, drop, and roll.
