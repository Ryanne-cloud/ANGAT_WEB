How To Survive in Tornado

1. Listen to the radio.
2. Take shelter in the cabinet, under the
table, or in the bathroom.
3. Stay away from windows and doors.

If you’re caught outside
1. Get as low to the ground as possible and
lie down in a ditch, for example.

Driving
2. If you’re heading away from the tornado path,
look for a building to shelter in.
2. If the tornado is too close, immediately get out
of the car and get down on the ground.

72 hours of survival without heat and electricity
1. Emergency supplies
2. Make photocopies of important documents.
3. Home inventory list
